A little about me


I’m a freelance marketing and PR consultant based in Leeds. I have nearly 10 years of experience in the marketing communications field.


Before I decided to take the big leap into freelancing in 2021, I gained experience in several in-house and agency roles, including as Head of Marketing at a boutique law firm, overseeing the marketing for the firms’ offices in Manchester, Yorkshire and London; and as an Account Manager and Content and PR Manager at a couple of PR agencies in Leeds and Huddersfield.


During my career so far, I’ve worked on projects across an extensive range of sectors for UK based SMEs including: marketing, tech, third sector, hospitality, wedding and events, circular economy, health, wellness, manufacturing, professional services and various retailers including fashion, children’s products, handmade products, beverages and more.
